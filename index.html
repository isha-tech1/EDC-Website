WHAT WE DO


We make the entrepreneurial world accessible to all the group of people who are a part of BBIT ecosystem.

Organise meetups

We host impactful meetups to bring together entrepreneurs and investors in a collaborative environment. These sessions provide a unique opportunity for startups to engage with potential investors, share innovative ideas, and foster meaningful connections. Our meetups focus on creating a conducive atmosphere for networking, enabling entrepreneurs to pitch their ventures and receive valuable feedback. By facilitating direct interaction, these gatherings play a pivotal role in building relationships that can lead to successful partnerships. Join our meetups to connect with like-minded individuals, explore synergies, and take significant strides in transforming your startup vision into a reality through fruitful collaborations with investors.

Provide training sessions

We conduct specialized training sessions to empower entrepreneurs in refining their pitching skills, understanding investor expectations, and navigating the intricacies of securing investments. These sessions offer practical insights, fostering a strategic approach to communication. Our goal is to equip startups with the knowledge and tools needed to articulate their vision persuasively. Through interactive training, we ensure participants gain a comprehensive understanding of the investment landscape. Join us in these sessions to enhance your capabilities, making your startup more compelling to potential investors and increasing the likelihood of turning your entrepreneurial aspirations into successful ventures.

Investors for startup

We organize events dedicated to connecting startups with investors. These gatherings offer a platform for entrepreneurs to pitch their innovative ideas, fostering networking and strategic communication. Our focus is on guiding startups to navigate the complexities of securing investments by providing insights into investor expectations. Through collaboration and mentorship, our events aim to bridge the gap between startups and investors, cultivating a dynamic ecosystem that fuels innovation and economic growth. Join us in transforming entrepreneurial visions into realities through meaningful investor connections.
